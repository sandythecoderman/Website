The Super RAG Engine (sRAG): The Knowledge & Reasoning Core

The Problem with Standard RAG

Standard RAG is like a librarian who can only fetch pages from books when you ask a question. It's helpful, but limited:

  • Often grabs the wrong page or an incomplete one
  • Doesn't understand relationships between different sources
  • Can't handle complex, multi-step questions
  • Answers are only as good as raw text, leading to errors

How Our Super RAG is Different

Our Super RAG is like a senior researcher who has read every book, document, and note in the library. It doesn't just fetch pages; it has built its own interconnected "map" of all the knowledge.

Unified Knowledge Graph Creation

Ingests everything—technical docs, APIs, databases, diagrams—and builds a deep, contextual knowledge graph

Real-Time Contextual Awareness

Continuously integrates live data streams, ensuring understanding is always current, never stale

Deep Relational Reasoning

Understands relationships, dependencies, and functions across your entire business ecosystem

Precision for LLMs

Drastically reduces AI hallucinations by providing structured, filtered, and relevant context

2

The Man-O-Man Engine: The Action & Orchestration Engine

If Super RAG is the brain that creates the plan, Man-O-Man is the sophisticated nervous system that executes that plan in the digital world, without human intervention.

Autonomous Workflow Execution

Receives high-level plans from sRAG and breaks them down into concrete, executable actions

Dynamic Tool Use

Interacts with vast arrays of tools and systems using their APIs—Jira, GitHub, AWS, Slack, and more

Seamless Systems Integration

Acts as a universal adapter, connecting to the digital infrastructure you already use

Feedback Loop

Results of actions are fed back into Super RAG, allowing the system to learn, adapt, and improve

How They Create Magic Together: The Skenzer Synapse Flywheel

Neither engine is truly revolutionary on its own. The magic happens when they work in a tight, continuous loop.

Example: A Critical Server Goes Down

1
Intent (Real-Time Detection)

Super RAG's real-time monitoring detects the server outage from live data streams

2
Reasoning (The Brain)

Super RAG consults its knowledge graph, understands the impact, and formulates a comprehensive remediation plan

3
Action (The Hands)

Man-O-Man executes the plan: reboots servers, runs diagnostics, creates tickets, and notifies teams

4
Result & Learning

Human engineers are notified with preliminary diagnostics done. The entire incident is recorded, making the system smarter for next time

This seamless cycle—from real-time understanding to autonomous action—is what allows us to move beyond simple automation and deliver true business autonomy.
